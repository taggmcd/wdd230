About Us

Scoots is a locally owned and operated scooter rental company ready to help you travel to your next adventure. We offer motor scooters, side-by-sides, and Jeeps. Get free deleivery of your rental to your hotel or Ainbnb when renting for 2 or more days. You also get unlimited milage and liability insurance.

Renting from Scoots is super quik and easy. Scoots welcomes locals, walk-in tourists, motor scooter enthusiasts, and cruise-based touring groups and partnerships. Scoots has two physical rental locations on Cozumel. One is near the Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock in el Centro de Cozumel and the other is near the Terminal Puerta Maya further south along the coast which caters to the cruise lines and patrons.
